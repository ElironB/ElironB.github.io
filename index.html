Workflow

From inbound lead to booked next step in four clear moves.

The workflow is simple on purpose. Sellable is there to handle the repetitive first-response layer, not force a bloated new operating model onto agents on day one.

01

Launch one listing into Sellable.

Set up the listing, tighten the copy, and get the buyer-facing number ready without rebuilding your whole workflow from scratch.

02

Start with the shared line, then add dedicated numbers only where they matter.

The included AI line covers the default flow first. Dedicated listing numbers stay optional for cleaner routing and clearer attribution.

03

Answer, qualify, and keep momentum moving.

Sellable handles the immediate back-and-forth, gathers context, and keeps serious buyers engaged instead of letting the lead stall out.

04

Step in strong when human judgment matters.

Agents focus on showings, negotiation, and decisions with context already captured instead of piecing the conversation together after the fact.

Technical footprint

The public product and the engineering story live together.

The Sellable codebase combines a web chat experience and a phone agent workflow. The current application is built in Next.js 16 with Supabase, VAPI, Telnyx, Nylas, Drizzle, and Polar.

Web chat agent

Streaming AI workflows for in-app guidance and agent tooling.

Voice agent

Webhook-based phone automation for inbound listing coverage.

Calendar and messaging

Nylas and Telnyx support scheduling and follow-up loops.

Database and billing

Supabase, Drizzle, and Polar keep account and plan logic grounded.
